\section*{LLM Usage Statement}
\label{sec:llm-usage}

This paper involves Large Language Models in two distinct roles.

\paragraph{LLM as subject of study}
The experimental work evaluates the penetration testing capabilities of five
commercially available LLMs (see \S\ref{sec:setup}). All inference calls were
made through their respective official APIs under the providers' standard terms
of service. No model weights were fine-tuned; all models were used out-of-the-box
with identical tool schemas and prompt templates. Prompt templates, full
conversation transcripts, and per-phase token counts are released as part of
the artifact.

\paragraph{LLM as writing aid}
During manuscript preparation, the authors used Anthropic Claude and OpenAI
GPT-4-class models for: (i)~drafting candidate section outlines subsequently
edited by the authors; (ii)~copy-editing for grammar, clarity, and concision;
(iii)~consistency checks on terminology and notation. No section was written
end-to-end by an LLM. All claims, experimental results, related-work
positioning, and scientific interpretations are the authors' own. Every
LLM-suggested citation was verified by retrieving the cited work.
