<instructions>
IMPORTANT: Call ONE tool at a time. Do not attempt parallel or simultaneous tool calls.

## Step 1 — Discovery
Call arp_scan() to list live hosts on the network.

## Step 2 — Network scan
Call nmap_discovery(target="{{target_subnet}}") to scan the target network.
Read the Phase 1 deliverable: read_deliverable("01_graph_analysis.md")

## Step 3 — Service scans (one tool call per IP, one at a time)
For EACH IP found, call nmap_scan separately:
- For routers/OpenWrt: nmap_scan(target="<IP>", ports="22,23,80,443,8080,8291", skip_discovery=true)  ← port 23 (Telnet) is critical
- For Raspberry Pi / gateways: nmap_scan(target="<IP>", ports="22,23,80,443,1883,3000,8080,8083,8086,9090", skip_discovery=true)
- For ESP32 / sensors: nmap_scan(target="<IP>", ports="22,80,443,1883,5683,8080", skip_discovery=true)
- For YAML-known devices: nmap_scan(target="<IP>", ports="22,23,80,443,1883,8080") — always include port 23
- Always use skip_discovery=true for undocumented devices (they often block ICMP)
Scan each IP one at a time.

## Step 3b — Undocumented devices
For EVERY IP not in the YAML model (Phase 1 list), run:
nmap_scan(target="<IP>", ports="22,23,80,443,1883,8080,8443", skip_discovery=true)
Document results even if all ports are closed. Do NOT skip undocumented devices.

## Step 4 — Deep scans on interesting services
For devices with SSH: call ssh_audit(host) — one device at a time.
For devices with HTTP: call curl_headers(url) — one URL at a time.
For devices with MQTT (port 1883): call mqtt_listen(broker, topic="#", count=5, timeout=5).

## Step 5 — Compare and document
For each IP: match against YAML model → classify as confirmed, undocumented, or unreachable.

## Step 6 — Completion checklist (MANDATORY before save_deliverable)
Before calling save_deliverable, verify ALL boxes are checked:
- [ ] nmap_scan called for EVERY discovered IP
- [ ] ssh_audit called for EVERY device with port 22
- [ ] curl_headers called for EVERY device with port 80 or 443
- [ ] mqtt_listen called for EVERY device with port 1883
- [ ] Every undocumented device documented individually
If any box is unchecked, continue scanning before saving.
Fill the template completely with ALL scan results, then — and ONLY then — call save_deliverable. Do NOT call save_deliverable with an empty or partial template.
</instructions>
